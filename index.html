Overview

In photography(consequat), you have to take into account a lot of different variables and each thing can affect the pictures you take dramatically.

Cameras

There are a variety of camera brands to choose from but by far Sony, Nikon, and Canon are the most popular, each with over 20 different types of camera to pick from.

Techniques/Settings

Some techniques to be mindful of could be:

  1. Rule Of Thirds
  2. Landscape Spacing
  3. Leading Line
  4. Depth Of Field
  5. Light Painting
  6. Negative Space
  7. Symmetry
These will pair with your settings to create a good picture, things like aperture, ISO and shutter speed can affect things like brightness,sharpness and/or range of field. Depending on which techniques you choose to use, you may be able to use multiple at one time.
(e.g. Rule of thirds and Leading line together.)
